Messages from CEO

Messages from CEO

Since our establishment in 2011, DCI Indonesia (DCI) has been committed to providing the best data center for our customers. DCI has maintained 100% uptime performance and implemented Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IOT) for incident avoidance and improved our operational efficiency. We believe by focusing on operational excellence, DCI would be able to realize our vision and mission of supporting the Indonesian digital economy.

Toto Sugiri
